DEPARTMENTS

1. Department of Computer Science & Engineering Focuses on areas like artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, data science, and software engineering. Offers B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. programs.

2. Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering Covers power systems, control systems, renewable energy, and embedded systems. Active in both academic research and industry collaboration.

3. Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering Core areas: VLSI design, signal processing, communication systems, IoT. Offers both UG and PG programs.

4. Department of Mechanical Engineering Covers thermal engineering, design, robotics, and manufacturing. Emphasizes industry-driven research and practical training.

5. Department of Civil Engineering Focuses on structural engineering, construction management, transportation, and environmental engineering.

6. Department of Architecture Offers Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) and design-focused courses. Promotes sustainable and vernacular design practices.

7. Department of Management (MBA) Provides MBA and research in areas such as finance, marketing, operations, and HR. Regular industry interaction and seminars.

8. Department of Applied Science & Humanities Supports foundational subjects like mathematics, physics, chemistry, economics, and English. Strengthens the academic base for engineering students.

9. Department of Information Technology Focuses on IT systems, networking, cloud computing, and system security.

10. Interdisciplinary Centres / Schools (Optional but recommended for KTU) KTU has established special research and academic hubs such as: Centre for Research & Innovation Centre for E-Governance Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) School of Computing, Engineering Design, and Automation (in some campuses/affiliates)
